Sylvester Mobley

I am a father, husband, son and brother.
I am a social entrepreneur, technologist and educator.
I am veteran of the war in Iraq who served in three branches of the military.
I am the founder of Coded by Kids and Coded by U.

02

What I Do

I lead a tech education nonporfit that teaches software engineering,
coding and digital design to underserved and underrepresented children.
I lead a teach education for profit company that provides adults with
project based programs designed to help them gain real world tech skills.
I design and manage project based tech education programs.
I teach kids to build cool stuff.
I speak about tech, education, diversity and sometimes all three at once.
